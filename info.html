BlockBird is a powerful and user-friendly Chrome extension designed to enhance your blockchain exploration experience. Here's what BlockBird can do:

  • Wallets: This can be used to store your wallet addresses. Very useful for quick copy to paste.
  • Scanner: Scans the current webpage for the keywords "airdrop", "points", and "rewards", then displays the count of occurrences and highlights each occurrence on the webpage.
  • Explorer: Provides a searchable list of blockchains with direct links to their respective block explorers.
  • and more to come...
