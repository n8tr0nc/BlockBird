BlockBird is a powerful and user-friendly Chrome extension designed to enhance your blockchain exploration experience. Here's what BlockBird can do:

  • Site Scanner: Scans the current webpage for the keywords "airdrop", "points", and "rewards", and displays the count of occurrences.
  • Block Explorer Directory: Provides a searchable list of blockchains with direct links to their respective block explorers.
