BlockBird is a powerful and user-friendly Chrome browser extension designed to enhance your Web3 experience. It includes a public wallet address manager, a smart webpage scanner that detects potential airdrops and point systems, and a searchable directory of blockchain explorers, all in one sleek interface.

Check It Out
Install BlockBird Chrome Browser Extension

Wallet Manager

BlockBird’s Wallet Manager allows you to securely store and organize your cryptocurrency wallet addresses with ease. Each wallet can be labeled for easy identification, making it simple to keep track of multiple accounts across different blockchains. You can quickly copy and paste any address with a single click, streamlining transactions or logins. Editing wallet details is just as easy — update labels or addresses at any time. Plus, with drag-and-drop reordering, you can arrange your wallets in any sequence that suits your workflow. Whether you're managing two wallets or twenty, BlockBird gives you a fast, intuitive way to stay organized and efficient.

Site Scanner

BlockBird’s Webpage Scanner helps you uncover hidden opportunities by scanning the current webpage for key terms like 'airdrop,' 'points,' and 'rewards.' With just one click, the scanner analyzes on-page content and highlights where these keywords appear, saving you the time and effort of manual searching. This tool is especially useful for discovering token distributions, engagement campaigns, or loyalty systems that may not be immediately visible. It provides a simple report showing how often each term is mentioned, and clearly marks them within the page for quick reference. Whether you're airdrop hunting or researching new platforms, the Scanner equips you with instant insights to help you stay ahead.
