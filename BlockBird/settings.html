Right-click on the extension icon in the browser toolbar and select "Open side panel". This will allow you to navigate through different tabs and websites while keeping your BlockBird always visible.
BlockBird is a community built, powerful and user-friendly browser extension for Chrome, Brave, and Kiwi. It is designed to enhance your blockchain exploration experience.
BlockBird may contain affiliate links to third-party websites or tools, which means we might earn a small commission if you use them at no extra cost to you. These links are included only when we believe they offer value to our users, and any support through them helps sustain and improve the BlockBird extension.
