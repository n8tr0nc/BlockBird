Easily manage your crypto wallets for quick copy/paste

1. Add single wallets by entering a label and the wallet address. Then click the add button (+).

2. Add multiple wallets by clicking the import button and selecting a .CSV file with one "label,wallet address" per line.

3. Export your labeled wallet addresses by clicking the export button, slecting a location, naming the file, and clicking save.

4. Drag and drop your wallets to reorder them.

5. Click the copy button () to copy a wallet address for pasting.

6. Click the edit button () to change a wallet label or address.

7. Click the delete button () to delete a wallet from your list.
